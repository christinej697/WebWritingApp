DLR 1: Dispelling the Localized Misconception

When beginning to study circuit analysis, it is not uncommon to have a very “localized” conception on how an electric circuit works. In such an outlook, one is inclined to think that if a change is made somewhere in the circuit, only the element that is changed is impacted. Let's spend a couple of minutes making certain we look at a circuit as a system and not simply a connection of independent elements.
